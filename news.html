The Swiss Yodel Choir Matterhorn is celebrating its 50th Anniversary this year! The main event will be an
Evening of Swiss Entertainment at the Hungarian Community Centre, Wantirna on 16th November 2019.
Please head to our Events page to find out more about this special evening.
We will also hold a Raffle with a chance to win one of ten great prizes. The sale of Raffle Tickets will open on 17 August and run until 16 November 2019.
Go to our Raffle page to find out more about the prizes and how to purchase tickets.
The Commemorative Booklet we have produced to for the occasion of the 50-Year Anniversary of the Matterhorn Choir has now been completed.
Printed copies of this booklet will be available at the 50 Years Matterhorn Celebration Evening on 16 November.
